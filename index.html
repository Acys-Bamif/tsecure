Who are we?

Founded in 2017 by group of friends, JSecurity has come a long way from its beginnings in Los Angeles. When our team first started out, their passion for making Internet better place drove them to quit day job so that JSecurity can offer you the world's best cyber security solutions. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own agency.
